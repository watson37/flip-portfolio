Conversation prompts for the evaluator

These prompts can help guide a more natural conversation about the work, rather than a formal scripted walkthrough.

Student Experience

  • Ask about the College 101 student message and why that moment mattered.
  • Ask how Sarah uses communication, feedback, and low-stakes activities like Padlet to build belonging.
  • Ask which teaching strategies, including H5P, escape rooms, or scaffolded assignments, felt most effective this year.
  • Ask how course structure and flexibility supported diverse learners while still holding high expectations.

Course Design

  • Ask about the redesign of Cents & Sensibility and how it became more practical and student-friendly.
  • Ask about the College 101 refresh, especially the streamlined competencies and increased use of formative activities.
  • Ask how LearnWise, the Cengage AI assistant, Sora, or Python learning fit into Sarah’s course design choices.
  • Ask which course artifact best captures growth in curriculum design this year.

Broader Impact

  • Ask about WTCS Voices and what Sarah learned from presenting beyond the college.
  • Ask how dual credit mentoring and high school instruction extended her impact across learner groups.
  • Ask how scholarship review, faculty mentoring, or advisory work supported students and the college in less visible ways.
  • Ask how community leadership, including City Council and MOSAIC work, connects back to her values as an educator.
