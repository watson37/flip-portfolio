15-minute talk flow

A simple structure you can use while clicking through the page or speaking alongside it.

0–2 min
Set the frame
Try: “I pulled a few highlights from each main area so I could focus on what mattered most this year, rather than walking through every line of the portfolio.”
2–5 min
Student Experience
Try: “In teaching, one of my biggest priorities was building a learning environment where students felt supported, challenged, and able to grow.”
5–9 min
Course Design
Try: “In course design, I focused on making learning more practical, more intuitive, and more connected to how students actually use these skills.”
9–12 min
Broader Impact
Try: “Outside the classroom, I looked for ways to extend that same student-centered focus through collaboration, mentoring, and community engagement.”
12–15 min
Close with the throughline
Try: “Across all three areas, the throughline for me was really intentional design, creating experiences and systems that help students feel more confident, more capable, and more connected.”
Closing line idea
Overall, this year was less about adding more and more about being intentional, designing learning experiences and systems that help students feel supported, capable, and successful.
